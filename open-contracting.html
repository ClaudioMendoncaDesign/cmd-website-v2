About Me

I am a digital designer with more than 10 years of experience. Since 2010, I focused my efforts on the thriving field of Open Government, where I have the pleasure to work with many of brightest people in the field on the quest to reinvent government.

Open Contracting

I worked with the Open Contracting Partnership team creating and maintaining the brand and all of it's graphic material.

The overarching goal of this initiative is to increase access of information on public contracting, from pre-award activities all the way through implementation and monitoring. In other words, it would make it easier for the population to know how public money is being spent by governments.
